## Launch Content Phases

Every launch has three phases. Each has different content goals.

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Build Anticipation)
Goal: Prime the audience. Make them aware something is coming and curious about what it is.
Duration: 1–4 weeks before launch (based on scope)

Content types:
- Behind-the-scenes content (building in public, the "why" story)
- Problem content (posts about the problem the product solves — before mentioning the solution)
- Teaser content (hints, partial reveals, countdowns)
- Waitlist / early access content (drive sign-ups before launch)
- Social proof setup (early user stories, beta feedback)

### Phase 2: Launch (Announcement + Amplification)
Goal: Get the announcement in front of as many relevant people as possible, as fast as possible.
Duration: Launch day + 3–5 days

Content types:
- The announcement post (hero piece — all platforms)
- Feature deep-dives (one per major feature or benefit)
- Social proof / testimonials (publish on launch week)
- FAQ content (answer objections before they're raised)
- Behind-the-scenes launch day content (authentic, real-time)
- Press/partner content (if applicable)

### Phase 3: Post-Launch (Extend & Convert)
Goal: Capture late movers, convert fence-sitters, build the story.
Duration: 2–6 weeks post-launch

Content types:
- Results content ("X people joined in the first week")
- Story content (user stories, outcomes, before/after)
- Objection handling posts (addressing the reasons people haven't tried it yet)
- "Still available" / urgency content (if limited offer)
- Lessons learned / behind-the-scenes retrospective

## Launch Brief Interview

Ask only what's not already in brand-voice:
- What is being launched? (product / feature / service / community / content)
- What is the launch date?
- What's the primary audience? (existing audience / new audience / both)
- What platforms will the launch be on?
- Is there a waitlist / early access period?
- What are the 3 key benefits or features?
- What objections will the audience have?
- Is there an offer element? (discount / bonus / limited access)
- What's the conversion goal? (signups / purchases / downloads)
